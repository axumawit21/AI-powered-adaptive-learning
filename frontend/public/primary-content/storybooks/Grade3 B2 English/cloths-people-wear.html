Vocabulary Words

  • purposes

    Definition: The reasons why you do something or why something is used.

    Example: We use different tools for different purposes, like a hammer for nails and a screwdriver for screws.

  • protect

    Definition: To keep someone or something safe from danger or harm.

    Example: Sunscreen helps to protect your skin from sunburn.

  • condition

    Definition: The state or way something is.

    Example: The bike was in excellent condition after its repair.

  • reflect

    Definition: To send light or heat back from a surface.

    Example: Shiny surfaces like mirrors reflect light very well.

  • heavy

    Definition: Weighing a lot; not easy to lift.

    Example: The big rock was too heavy for me to move alone.

  • thick

    Definition: Having a large distance between its two sides; not thin.

    Example: I wore a thick sweater to stay warm in the snow.

  • sharp

    Definition: Having a very thin edge or a fine point that can cut or prick easily.

    Example: Be careful with that knife, it has a very sharp blade.
