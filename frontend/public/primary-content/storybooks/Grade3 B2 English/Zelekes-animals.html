Vocabulary Words

  • countryside

    Definition: The land outside of cities and towns, often with farms and open fields.

    Example: We went for a long walk in the peaceful countryside, far away from all the noise.

  • breeds

    Definition: To raise animals so they can have babies.

    Example: The farmer breeds special chickens to lay extra large eggs.

  • domestic

    Definition: Animals that are kept by people, not wild.

    Example: Dogs and cats are common domestic animals that live with us in our homes.

  • purposes

    Definition: The reasons for which something is done or used.

    Example: My little brother uses his toy truck for many purposes, like carrying blocks and small cars.

  • transportation

    Definition: The act of moving people or things from one place to another.

    Example: Buses and trains are forms of public transportation that many people use.

  • thresh

    Definition: To separate grains of wheat or other crops from the rest of the plant.

    Example: Long ago, farmers would hit the dry stalks to thresh the grain by hand.

  • ploughing

    Definition: Turning over the soil in a field before planting seeds.

    Example: The tractor spent all morning ploughing the field, getting it ready for the new crops.
