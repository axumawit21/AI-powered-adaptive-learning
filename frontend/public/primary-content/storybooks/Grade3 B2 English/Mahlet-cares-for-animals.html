Vocabulary Words

  • regularly

    Definition: Done often or at the same time each day or week.

    Example: I visit my grandparents regularly on weekends.

  • provides

    Definition: Gives something that is needed.

    Example: The teacher provides help to all the students.

  • improves

    Definition: Makes something better.

    Example: Eating healthy food improves your energy.

  • products

    Definition: Things that are made or come from something else.

    Example: Many tasty products are made from corn, like popcorn and tortillas.

  • leather

    Definition: A strong material made from animal skin, often used for shoes, bags, or jackets.

    Example: My dad wears a cool leather jacket when it's cold.

  • wool

    Definition: The soft, thick hair from sheep that is used to make warm clothes and blankets.

    Example: I have a cozy blanket made from soft wool.
