Vocabulary Words

  • seasons

    Definition: Different times of the year with special kinds of weather, like summer or winter.

    Example: My favorite season is autumn because I love to jump in piles of leaves.

  • plough

    Definition: To dig up and turn over the soil in a field, usually before planting seeds.

    Example: The farmer used a large machine to plough his land before putting in the new plants.

  • sow

    Definition: To put seeds in the ground so they can grow into plants.

    Example: We will sow a lot of flower seeds in our garden next week.

  • crops

    Definition: Plants that farmers grow in large amounts to eat or sell, like corn, wheat, or rice.

    Example: The rain helped all the crops in the field grow big and healthy.

  • shortage

    Definition: When there is not enough of something that is needed.

    Example: There was a cookie shortage in the house after the party.

  • daytime

    Definition: The part of the day when the sun is up and it is light outside.

    Example: Most animals are awake and looking for food during the daytime.

  • evening

    Definition: The end of the day, when the sun starts to go down and it gets dark.

    Example: My family likes to watch movies together in the evening.
