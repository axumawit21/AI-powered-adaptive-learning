Vocabulary Words

  • celebrate

    Definition: To do something special and fun because it's an important day or event.

    Example: We will celebrate my birthday with cake and games.

  • national

    Definition: Something that belongs to or is about a whole country.

    Example: The flag is a national symbol of our country.

  • religious

    Definition: Having to do with a belief in God or gods and the way people show that belief.

    Example: They went to church for a special religious service.

  • considered

    Definition: Thought about in a certain way, or believed to be.

    Example: Helping others is considered a kind act.

  • exchange

    Definition: To give something to someone and receive something back from them.

    Example: The two friends decided to exchange toys for the afternoon.

  • relatives

    Definition: People who are part of your family, like aunts, uncles, or cousins.

    Example: All our relatives came over for Thanksgiving dinner.

  • support

    Definition: To help someone or something, often when they need it.

    Example: My friends always support me when I try new things.
