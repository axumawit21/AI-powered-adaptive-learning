Vocabulary Words

  • Sharp

    Definition: Having a very thin edge or a fine point that can cut or prick easily.

    Example: Be careful when you use scissors because they are very sharp.

  • Objects

    Definition: Any non-living thing that you can see and touch.

    Example: The classroom was filled with many different objects, like books and pencils.

  • Careful

    Definition: Paying close attention to what you are doing so you don't make mistakes or get hurt.

    Example: It is important to be careful when crossing the street.

  • Bleeds

    Definition: When blood comes out from a cut or wound on your body.

    Example: If you fall and scrape your knee, it might start to bleed a little.

  • Absent

    Definition: Not present; away from a place where you are expected to be.

    Example: Sarah was absent from school today because she had a doctor's appointment.

  • Advises

    Definition: To tell someone what you think they should do, often to help them.

    Example: My teacher always advises us to study hard for our tests.

  • Razor blades

    Definition: A very thin, sharp piece of metal used for cutting or shaving.

    Example: Adults use razor blades to shave, but they are very dangerous for children to touch.
