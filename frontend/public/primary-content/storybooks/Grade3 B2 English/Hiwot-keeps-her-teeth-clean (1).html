Vocabulary Words

  • countryside

    Definition: The land outside of cities, often with farms and open fields.

    Example: My grandparents live in the quiet countryside where there are lots of trees and animals.

  • only child

    Definition: A child who does not have any brothers or sisters.

    Example: Leo is an only child, so he has all his toys to himself.

  • health

    Definition: The state of being strong and not sick.

    Example: Eating fruits and vegetables is important for good health.

  • local

    Definition: Belonging to or found in a particular place near you.

    Example: We buy fresh bread from the local bakery every morning.

  • remove

    Definition: To take something away or off.

    Example: Please remove your muddy shoes before you come inside the house.

  • remains

    Definition: The parts of something that are left after the rest has been used or taken away.

    Example: After we ate the cake, only a few crumbs were the remains on the plate.

  • advice

    Definition: Ideas or suggestions given to help someone.

    Example: My mom gave me good advice about how to share my toys with my friends.
