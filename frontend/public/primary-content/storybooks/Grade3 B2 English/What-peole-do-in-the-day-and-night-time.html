Vocabulary Words

  • except

    Definition: Meaning 'but not' or 'other than.' It's used when you are talking about everything or everyone besides one thing or person.

    Example: Everyone went to the party except for John, who was sick.

  • activities

    Definition: Things that people do, especially for a particular purpose or for enjoyment.

    Example: Playing games and reading books are fun activities to do indoors.

  • harvest

    Definition: To gather crops that have grown, like picking apples or pulling carrots out of the ground.

    Example: The family helped to harvest all the ripe strawberries from their garden.

  • crops

    Definition: Plants like fruits, vegetables, or grains that farmers grow to eat or sell.

    Example: Corn and wheat are important crops that grow in many fields.

  • merchants

    Definition: People who buy and sell things to make money, like shopkeepers or traders.

    Example: At the market, merchants displayed their goods for everyone to see.

  • goods

    Definition: Things that are made or grown to be sold, like toys, clothes, or food.

    Example: The delivery truck carried many different goods to the store.

  • tasks

    Definition: Small jobs or duties that you need to do.

    Example: My teacher gave us a list of tasks to complete before the end of class.

  • usually

    Definition: Most of the time, or almost always.

    Example: It usually rains more in the spring than in the summer.
