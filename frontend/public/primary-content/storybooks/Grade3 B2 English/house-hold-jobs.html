Vocabulary Words

  • village

    Definition: A very small town, usually in the countryside.

    Example: My grandmother lives in a peaceful village with green fields all around.

  • repairs

    Definition: Fixes something that is broken or damaged.

    Example: The mechanic repairs cars so they can drive safely again.

  • sweeps

    Definition: Cleans a floor or surface using a broom.

    Example: Every morning, the janitor sweeps the school hallways.

  • weeds

    Definition: Wild plants that grow where they are not wanted, often hurting other plants.

    Example: We had to pull out all the weeds from our garden so the vegetables could grow big.

  • hoe

    Definition: A tool with a long handle and a flat blade, used for digging or removing weeds in a garden.

    Example: The farmer used a hoe to prepare the soil for planting new seeds.

  • spinner

    Definition: A person whose job is to turn fibers like cotton or wool into thread or yarn.

    Example: The spinner made soft yarn that was used to knit a warm blanket.

  • spins

    Definition: To twist fibers together to make thread or yarn.

    Example: The silkworm spins a cocoon to protect itself.
