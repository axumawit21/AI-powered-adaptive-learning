Vocabulary Words

  • village

    Definition: A very small town, often in the countryside.

    Example: My grandparents live in a peaceful village surrounded by fields.

  • rainy season

    Definition: A time of year when it rains a lot every day.

    Example: During the rainy season, we always carry umbrellas.

  • market

    Definition: A place where people go to buy and sell many different things, like food or clothes.

    Example: We went to the market to buy fresh vegetables.

  • choose

    Definition: To pick one thing from a group of many options.

    Example: You can choose any book you like from the shelf.

  • trousers

    Definition: A piece of clothing that covers both legs, also called pants.

    Example: He wore his new blue trousers to school.

  • woolen

    Definition: Made from wool, which is soft and warm, usually from sheep.

    Example: My cozy blanket is made of woolen material.

  • jacket

    Definition: A warm coat worn on the upper part of your body.

    Example: She zipped up her jacket because it started to get cold.
