Vocabulary Words

  • workplaces

    Definition: Places where people go to do their jobs.

    Example: Many different workplaces can be found in a big city.

  • clinic

    Definition: A small hospital or place where doctors help sick people.

    Example: I went to the clinic to get a shot and feel better.

  • treats

    Definition: To care for someone who is sick or hurt to help them get better.

    Example: The kind doctor treats all the children with a smile.

  • government

    Definition: The group of people who lead a country or a town.

    Example: The government helps build new parks and schools for everyone.

  • community

    Definition: All the people who live in the same area or town.

    Example: Our community held a special event at the town square.

  • serves

    Definition: To bring food or drinks to someone, especially in a restaurant.

    Example: The kind waiter serves delicious pizza to his customers.

  • manages

    Definition: To be in charge of something or to control how it works.

    Example: The zookeeper manages all the animals and makes sure they are healthy.
