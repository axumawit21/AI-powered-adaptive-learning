Vocabulary Words

  • students

    Definition: Children or adults who go to school to learn.

    Example: The students eagerly raised their hands to answer the teacher's question.

  • uniforms

    Definition: Special matching clothes that all members of a group or school wear.

    Example: The soccer team wore their bright red uniforms for the game.

  • return

    Definition: To go back to a place where you were before.

    Example: After visiting the park, we will return home for dinner.

  • change

    Definition: To take off one set of clothes and put on another.

    Example: Maya had to change her wet clothes after playing in the rain.

  • dirty

    Definition: Not clean; covered with dust, mud, or stains.

    Example: My hands were dirty after digging in the garden.

  • clean

    Definition: Free from dirt or stains; neat.

    Example: My mom asked me to keep my room clean and tidy.

  • listens

    Definition: Hears what someone says and pays attention to it.

    Example: The puppy always listens carefully when its owner calls its name.
