Vocabulary Words

  • playful

    Definition: Liking to play and have fun.

    Example: The playful kitten jumped on all the toys.

  • lost

    Definition: Not knowing where something or someone is.

    Example: I was sad when my favorite crayon got lost.

  • near

    Definition: Close to something.

    Example: The squirrel buried its nuts near the old fence.

  • under

    Definition: Below something.

    Example: We put the picnic blanket under the shady tree.

  • calls

    Definition: To shout out a name or word loudly.

    Example: My teacher calls out our names when she takes attendance.
