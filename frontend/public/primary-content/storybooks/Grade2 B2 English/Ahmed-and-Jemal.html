Vocabulary Words

  • sweeps

    Definition: To clean a floor or ground using a broom or brush.

    Example: My grandma always sweeps the porch after breakfast.

  • washes

    Definition: To clean something with water and sometimes soap.

    Example: She washes her hands before she eats her lunch.

  • compound

    Definition: An area of land that has buildings and is surrounded by a fence or wall.

    Example: The children played soccer in the large compound behind their school.

  • pet

    Definition: To gently stroke or touch an animal, often as a sign of affection.

    Example: The kind boy likes to pet the friendly dog when he sees it.

  • sort

    Definition: To put things into groups based on their type, size, or other qualities.

    Example: We helped the teacher sort the crayons by color.

  • gate

    Definition: A movable barrier in a fence or wall that you can open and close to go through.

    Example: Please close the garden gate so the rabbit doesn't get out.
