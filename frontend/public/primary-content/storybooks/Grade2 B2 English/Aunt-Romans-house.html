Vocabulary Words

  • apartment

    Definition: A home that is part of a larger building, often with many other homes.

    Example: My best friend lives in a big apartment building downtown.

  • sweeps

    Definition: To clean a floor or surface using a broom to push away dirt.

    Example: The kind janitor sweeps the school hallways every evening.

  • mends

    Definition: To fix something that is broken or damaged.

    Example: My grandma mends my socks when they get a hole in them.

  • sponge

    Definition: A soft material with many holes that can soak up water, used for cleaning.

    Example: I used a wet sponge to wipe the muddy paw prints off the floor.

  • pan

    Definition: A round, flat metal container used for cooking food on a stove.

    Example: My dad cooked delicious pancakes in a large frying pan.

  • towel

    Definition: A piece of soft cloth used for drying things, like hands or hair.

    Example: After my shower, I grabbed a clean towel to dry myself off.

  • pillow

    Definition: A soft cushion that you rest your head on when you are sleeping.

    Example: I like to fluff my pillow to make it extra comfy before I go to sleep.
