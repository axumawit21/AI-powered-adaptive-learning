Vocabulary Words

  • Excited

    Definition: Feeling very happy and full of energy about something.

    Example: The children were very excited about going to the park.

  • Flown

    Definition: To have traveled through the air, usually in an airplane or like a bird.

    Example: The little bird had flown far away from its nest.

  • Airport

    Definition: A place where airplanes take off and land, and where people get on and off planes.

    Example: We went to the airport to pick up Grandma when her plane landed.

  • Arrive

    Definition: To reach a place after a journey.

    Example: The mail carrier will arrive at our house soon with the letters.

  • Village

    Definition: A very small town, usually in the countryside.

    Example: My aunt lives in a quiet village with only a few houses.

  • Countryside

    Definition: The land outside of cities and towns, often with farms, fields, and forests.

    Example: We drove through the beautiful countryside to see the mountains.

  • Row

    Definition: To move a boat through water using oars.

    Example: The two friends decided to row their small boat across the pond.
