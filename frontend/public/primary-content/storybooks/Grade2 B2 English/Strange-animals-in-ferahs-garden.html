Vocabulary Words

  • Strange

    Definition: Not normal or what you usually see.

    Example: The cloud in the sky had a strange shape like a dragon.

  • Garden

    Definition: A piece of land, often near a house, where flowers, vegetables, or fruit are grown.

    Example: My grandma loves to water the roses in her garden.

  • Goat

    Definition: An animal with horns and a beard, often kept for its milk or meat.

    Example: The baby goat was jumping and playing in the field.

  • Barks

    Definition: The loud sound a dog makes.

    Example: My neighbor's dog barks every time someone walks by.

  • Monkey

    Definition: A furry animal that lives in trees, often has a long tail, and is good at climbing.

    Example: We saw a funny monkey swinging from branch to branch at the zoo.

  • Catch

    Definition: To grab or hold something that is moving, especially after it has been thrown.

    Example: Can you catch the ball when I throw it to you?

  • Cabbage patch

    Definition: A small area of ground where cabbage vegetables are grown.

    Example: The farmer harvested fresh cabbages from the cabbage patch.
