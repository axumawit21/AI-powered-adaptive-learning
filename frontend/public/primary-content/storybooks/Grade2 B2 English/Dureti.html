Vocabulary Words

  • uniform

    Definition: A special set of clothes that everyone in a group wears, like for school or a job.

    Example: The baker wore a white uniform while making bread.

  • school

    Definition: A place where children go to learn new things and play with friends.

    Example: We learn about animals at school.

  • takes

    Definition: To carry or bring something from one place to another.

    Example: My mom takes her book to the park to read.

  • bag

    Definition: A container made of soft material used for carrying things.

    Example: I put my toys in a big bag when I go to my friend's house.

  • pink

    Definition: A light red color, often seen in flowers or candy.

    Example: The sky turned a beautiful pink at sunset.
