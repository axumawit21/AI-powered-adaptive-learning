Vocabulary Words

  • jacket

    Definition: A coat or outer piece of clothing worn on the upper part of your body.

    Example: I put on my warm jacket before I went outside to play.

  • scarf

    Definition: A piece of cloth worn around the neck, head, or shoulders for warmth or style.

    Example: She wrapped a colorful scarf around her neck when it was cold.

  • trousers

    Definition: A piece of clothing that covers your legs and is worn from the waist down.

    Example: My dad wears smart trousers to work every day.

  • ties

    Definition: Long pieces of cloth worn around the neck under a shirt collar, often by boys or men.

    Example: For the school concert, all the boys wore red ties.

  • sweater

    Definition: A warm piece of clothing, usually made of wool, that covers the upper part of your body.

    Example: I love to wear my cozy sweater on chilly mornings.

  • skirt

    Definition: A piece of clothing worn by girls or women that hangs from the waist down.

    Example: The girl twirled around in her new sparkly skirt.
