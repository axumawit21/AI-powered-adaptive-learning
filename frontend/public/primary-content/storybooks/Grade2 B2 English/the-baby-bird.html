Vocabulary Words

  • obey

    Definition: To do what someone tells you to do.

    Example: My dog loves to obey my commands when I ask him to sit.

  • hunting

    Definition: Looking for food or other things.

    Example: We went hunting for pretty seashells on the beach.

  • finally

    Definition: After a long time or after many things have happened.

    Example: After waiting all day, the bus finally arrived.

  • cave

    Definition: A big hole in the side of a mountain or under the ground.

    Example: We saw bats sleeping upside down in the dark cave.

  • field

    Definition: A large area of open land, often covered in grass.

    Example: The cows were grazing peacefully in the green field.

  • beautiful

    Definition: Very pretty or lovely to look at or listen to.

    Example: The rainbow after the rain was so beautiful.

  • follows

    Definition: Goes after someone or something.

    Example: The little duckling always follows its mother in a line.
