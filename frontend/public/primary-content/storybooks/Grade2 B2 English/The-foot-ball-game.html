Vocabulary Words

  • light

    Definition: Not heavy; easy to lift.

    Example: The balloon was so light it floated away.

  • reach

    Definition: To arrive at a place.

    Example: We will reach the top of the hill soon.

  • careful

    Definition: Being aware and cautious to avoid danger or mistakes.

    Example: Be careful when you use scissors so you don't cut yourself.

  • toss

    Definition: To throw something gently or lightly.

    Example: Please toss me the book from the table.

  • herd

    Definition: To gather and guide a group of animals.

    Example: The cowboy used his horse to herd the cattle across the field.
