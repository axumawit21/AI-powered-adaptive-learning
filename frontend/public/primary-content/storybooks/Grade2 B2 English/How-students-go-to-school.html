Vocabulary Words

  • transportation

    Definition: Ways people or things move from one place to another.

    Example: Buses, cars, and trains are all types of transportation.

  • different

    Definition: Not the same; distinct or unlike something else.

    Example: My two socks are different colors, one is blue and one is red.

  • travel

    Definition: To go from one place to another.

    Example: Many birds travel south for the winter.

  • motorcycle

    Definition: A vehicle with two wheels, like a bicycle, but powered by an engine.

    Example: The man wore a helmet while riding his motorcycle down the road.

  • taxi

    Definition: A car with a driver that you pay to take you where you want to go.

    Example: We called a taxi to take us to the airport.
