Vocabulary Words

  • tall

    Definition: Being very high or a lot higher than average.

    Example: The giraffe was so tall it could eat leaves from the top of the tree.

  • short

    Definition: Not very high or not as high as others.

    Example: The short little dog had trouble jumping onto the big couch.

  • live

    Definition: To make your home in a place or to be alive.

    Example: Fish live in the water and need it to breathe.

  • together

    Definition: With or next to each other.

    Example: My friends and I like to play games together in the park.

  • happy

    Definition: Feeling or showing pleasure or joy.

    Example: She felt very happy when she got a new toy for her birthday.
