Vocabulary Words

  • roasts

    Definition: To cook food with dry heat, usually in an oven or over a fire.

    Example: Dad roasts marshmallows over the campfire.

  • maize

    Definition: Another word for corn, a tall plant with large seeds grown for food.

    Example: The farmer grew many rows of maize in his field.

  • finish

    Definition: To complete something or bring it to an end.

    Example: I will finish my drawing before bedtime.

  • reason

    Definition: Why something happens or why someone does something.

    Example: The reason I am happy is because it's my birthday.

  • together

    Definition: With another person or people; not alone.

    Example: The children built a tall tower together.

  • lake

    Definition: A large body of water surrounded by land.

    Example: We saw ducks swimming in the calm lake.

  • counts

    Definition: To say numbers in order, or to find out how many there are.

    Example: My little brother counts his fingers and toes.
