Vocabulary Words

  • bathroom

    Definition: A room in a house where you can wash your hands and body, and go to the toilet.

    Example: My mom helped me brush my teeth in the bathroom before bed.

  • kitchen

    Definition: A special room in a house where people cook food.

    Example: We made yummy cookies in the kitchen this afternoon.

  • stove

    Definition: A cooking machine that gets hot to make food ready to eat.

    Example: My dad cooked scrambled eggs on the stove for breakfast.

  • dining room

    Definition: A room in a house where everyone sits together to eat meals.

    Example: We set the table in the dining room for a special dinner.

  • reading room

    Definition: A quiet room in a house where people go to read books.

    Example: Grandma likes to sit in her reading room and enjoy a good book every evening.

  • bedroom

    Definition: The room in your house where you sleep at night.

    Example: I keep all my favorite toys in my bedroom.

  • family

    Definition: The people you live with who love you, like your parents, brothers, and sisters.

    Example: My family went on a fun picnic at the park last weekend.
