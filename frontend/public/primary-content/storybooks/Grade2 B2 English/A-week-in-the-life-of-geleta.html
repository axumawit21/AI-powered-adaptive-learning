Vocabulary Words

  • celebrate

    Definition: To have a party or do something special for an important day or event.

    Example: We will celebrate my birthday with cake and presents.

  • plans

    Definition: Ideas about what you want to do in the future.

    Example: My plans for the summer include swimming every day.

  • market

    Definition: A place where people go to buy and sell food and other things.

    Example: My grandmother buys fresh flowers at the market every Saturday.

  • fresh

    Definition: New and clean, or not old (especially for food).

    Example: The baker made fresh bread this morning.

  • church

    Definition: A building where people go to pray and worship.

    Example: Many families go to church together on Sunday mornings.
