Vocabulary Words

  • Countryside

    Definition: The land outside of cities and towns, often with farms and fields.

    Example: We drove through the beautiful countryside with green hills and many animals.

  • Path

    Definition: A narrow way made by people or animals walking over it, or a small road.

    Example: The children followed the secret path through the woods to find a hidden waterfall.

  • Herd

    Definition: A large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live and eat together.

    Example: A herd of elephants slowly walked across the grassy plain.

  • Bank

    Definition: The ground along the side of a river or lake.

    Example: We sat on the river bank and watched the ducks swim by.

  • Chattering

    Definition: Making quick, repetitive sounds, often like a monkey or teeth.

    Example: The squirrel was chattering loudly as it ran up the tree.

  • Nears

    Definition: To get close to something or someone.

    Example: As the sun nears the horizon, the sky turns beautiful colors.
