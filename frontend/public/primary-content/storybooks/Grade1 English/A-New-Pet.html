Vocabulary Words

  • wags

    Definition: Moves quickly from side to side.

    Example: The happy dog will wag its tail when its owner comes home.

  • washes

    Definition: Cleans something with water and soap.

    Example: My dad washes his car every Saturday morning.

  • feeds

    Definition: Gives food to someone or an animal.

    Example: She feeds her pet rabbit fresh carrots and lettuce.

  • vet

    Definition: A special doctor who takes care of animals.

    Example: We take our cat to the vet for its yearly check-up.

  • healthy

    Definition: Strong and not sick.

    Example: Eating vegetables and playing outside helps you stay healthy.
