Vocabulary Words

  • Cabbages

    Definition: A large, round, green vegetable with thick leaves.

    Example: My dad made a salad with fresh cabbages from the garden.

  • Peppers

    Definition: A colorful vegetable that can be sweet or spicy, often red, yellow, or green.

    Example: We sliced the bell peppers to put on our pizza.

  • Vegetables

    Definition: Parts of plants like carrots, broccoli, or lettuce that we eat to stay healthy.

    Example: Eating your vegetables helps you grow big and strong.

  • Healthy

    Definition: Being well and strong, not sick.

    Example: Drinking lots of water helps you stay healthy.

  • Strong

    Definition: Having power or force; able to lift heavy things or run fast.

    Example: The strong elephant could lift the big log with its trunk.

  • Everyday

    Definition: Something that happens or is done each day.

    Example: I read a book everyday before I go to sleep.
