Vocabulary Words

  • Like

    Definition: To think something is good or enjoy it very much.

    Example: I like to eat ice cream on a hot day.

  • Mangoes

    Definition: A sweet, juicy tropical fruit with a big seed in the middle.

    Example: My grandma cut up a fresh mango for dessert.

  • Colour

    Definition: What makes things look different, like red, blue, or yellow.

    Example: What is the colour of your new backpack?

  • Orange

    Definition: A bright color that looks like a pumpkin or a carrot.

    Example: The artist used a bright orange paint for the sunset.

  • Play

    Definition: To do fun things, often with toys, games, or other people.

    Example: The children love to play hide-and-seek in the garden.

  • Football

    Definition: A game where two teams try to kick a ball into the other team's goal.

    Example: My brother watches a football match every Sunday afternoon.
