Vocabulary Words

  • grapes

    Definition: Small, round fruits that grow in bunches and can be green, red, or purple.

    Example: My mom bought a big bunch of sweet grapes for a snack.

  • lemons

    Definition: Yellow fruits that taste very sour and are often used to flavor drinks or food.

    Example: I like to put a slice of lemon in my water to make it taste fresh.

  • mangoes

    Definition: Sweet, juicy tropical fruits with a large seed inside.

    Example: My favorite smoothie has a lot of mango in it because it's so sweet.

  • papayas

    Definition: Large, orange-colored tropical fruits with many small black seeds inside.

    Example: My grandpa grew a papaya tree in his backyard, and the fruits were delicious.

  • oranges

    Definition: Round, juicy fruits with a thick orange peel and a sweet, tangy taste.

    Example: I love to peel an orange and eat the juicy slices for breakfast.
