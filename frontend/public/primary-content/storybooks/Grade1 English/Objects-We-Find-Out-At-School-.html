Vocabulary Words

  • Objects

    Definition: Things you can see and touch.

    Example: My toy box is full of many colorful objects.

  • Classroom

    Definition: A room in a school where students learn.

    Example: We hung our drawings on the wall of our classroom.

  • Chalkboard

    Definition: A large, dark board used in schools for writing with chalk.

    Example: The teacher wrote the math problems on the chalkboard.

  • Marker

    Definition: A special pen that uses ink to write or draw.

    Example: I used a blue marker to draw a big circle.

  • Rulers

    Definition: Flat, straight tools used to measure things or draw straight lines.

    Example: We used our rulers to draw perfect squares in art class.

  • Different

    Definition: Not the same; unalike.

    Example: The two puppies looked different, one had spots and the other was all black.
