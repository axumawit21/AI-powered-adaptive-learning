Vocabulary Words

  • donkey

    Definition: An animal like a small horse, with long ears.

    Example: The farmer used a donkey to help carry heavy baskets to the market.

  • uncle

    Definition: Your father's or mother's brother.

    Example: My uncle told me a funny story about when he was a little boy.

  • sheep

    Definition: A farm animal covered in thick wool, kept for its wool.

    Example: The shepherd watched over his flock of sheep in the green pasture.

  • camels

    Definition: Large animals with humps on their backs that live in hot, dry places like deserts.

    Example: We saw several camels walking slowly in a long line across the sandy dunes.

  • animal

    Definition: A living creature that is not a plant.

    Example: My favorite animal at the zoo was the playful monkey swinging from the trees.
