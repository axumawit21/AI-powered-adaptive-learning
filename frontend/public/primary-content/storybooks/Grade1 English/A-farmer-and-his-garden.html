Vocabulary Words

  • countryside

    Definition: The land far away from a city, often with farms, fields, and trees.

    Example: We saw many cows grazing in the green countryside.

  • farmer

    Definition: A person who works on a farm, growing food or raising animals.

    Example: The farmer woke up early to feed his chickens.

  • grow

    Definition: To get bigger or taller, like a plant or a child.

    Example: My little brother loves to watch the sunflower seeds grow into tall plants.

  • everything

    Definition: All of the things or all that exists.

    Example: The toy box held everything from cars to building blocks.

  • cannot

    Definition: Not able to do something.

    Example: I cannot reach the cookie jar because it is too high.
