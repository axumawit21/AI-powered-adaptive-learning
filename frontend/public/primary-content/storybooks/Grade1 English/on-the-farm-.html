Vocabulary Words

  • donkeys

    Definition: An animal like a small horse with long ears and a loud "hee-haw" sound.

    Example: The farmer used the strong donkey to help carry heavy baskets.

  • moo

    Definition: The sound a cow makes.

    Example: We heard the cow moo loudly when it was time for dinner.

  • oxen

    Definition: Big, strong cows often used to pull carts or plows.

    Example: In olden days, oxen helped farmers pull their heavy wagons.

  • piglets

    Definition: Baby pigs.

    Example: The mother pig had many cute piglets playing in the mud.

  • field

    Definition: An open area of land, often covered in grass, where animals graze or crops grow.

    Example: We ran through the green field to get to the playground.

  • farm

    Definition: A place where people grow food and raise animals.

    Example: My grandma lives on a farm with many chickens and a big garden.
