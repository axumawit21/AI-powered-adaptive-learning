Vocabulary Words

  • picking

    Definition: Taking something, like fruit, from where it grows.

    Example: The children enjoyed picking strawberries from the farm.

  • garden

    Definition: A piece of land used for growing flowers, vegetables, or fruit.

    Example: My grandpa planted colorful flowers in his garden.

  • behind

    Definition: At the back of something.

    Example: The little cat hid behind the sofa.

  • each

    Definition: Every single one of two or more things or people.

    Example: Each student received a prize for their hard work.

  • sweet

    Definition: Having a pleasant taste like sugar.

    Example: The cake was so sweet and delicious.

  • mangoes

    Definition: A type of sweet, juicy fruit that grows in warm places.

    Example: I love to eat fresh mangoes in the summer.
