Vocabulary Words

  • healthy

    Definition: Good for your body and helps you stay strong.

    Example: Eating fresh fruits and vegetables helps you stay healthy.

  • dislike

    Definition: To not like something at all.

    Example: My cat will dislike it if you try to give her a bath.

  • buy

    Definition: To get something by giving money for it.

    Example: Mom said she would buy me a new book for my birthday.

  • dialogue

    Definition: A conversation or talk between two or more people.

    Example: The actors learned their dialogue before performing the play.

  • please

    Definition: A polite word you say when you ask for something.

    Example: May I have another slice of pizza, please?

  • try

    Definition: To taste something new or attempt to do something.

    Example: I want to try the new swing set at the park today.
