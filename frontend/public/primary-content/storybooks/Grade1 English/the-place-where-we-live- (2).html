Vocabulary Words

  • countryside

    Definition: An area of land with farms, fields, and trees, away from big cities.

    Example: We drove through the pretty countryside to visit Grandma.

  • farm

    Definition: A place where people grow food like vegetables or raise animals like cows and chickens.

    Example: The farmer worked hard on his farm every day.

  • older

    Definition: Having lived for more years than someone or something else.

    Example: My brother is older than me, so he knows how to ride a bike.

  • field

    Definition: A large open area of land, often used for growing plants or for animals to graze.

    Example: The children played soccer in the big green field.

  • water hole

    Definition: A natural pool of water where animals often go to drink.

    Example: The thirsty zebras gathered around the water hole.

  • behind

    Definition: At the back of something.

    Example: The dog hid behind the couch when it heard the loud noise.

  • road

    Definition: A path with a hard surface for cars and people to travel on.

    Example: We walked carefully along the quiet road.
