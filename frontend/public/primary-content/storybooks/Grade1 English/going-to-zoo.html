Vocabulary Words

  • roars

    Definition: Makes a very loud, deep sound, like a big cat.

    Example: The brave knight heard a dragon's terrible roar from inside the cave.

  • trunk

    Definition: A very long nose that an elephant uses to grab things, drink water, and spray water.

    Example: The baby elephant used its trunk to pick up a juicy apple.

  • tortoise

    Definition: A type of reptile with a hard shell that moves very slowly and lives on land.

    Example: We watched the old tortoise slowly eat a leaf in the garden.

  • slowly

    Definition: Moving or doing something without much speed.

    Example: The caterpillar slowly crawled up the big green leaf.

  • fur

    Definition: The soft, thick hair that covers the body of some animals.

    Example: I love to pet my dog's soft, warm fur.

  • hyena

    Definition: A wild animal that looks a bit like a dog and lives in Africa, known for its laugh-like sound.

    Example: We saw a picture of a hyena with its spotted coat in the jungle book.
