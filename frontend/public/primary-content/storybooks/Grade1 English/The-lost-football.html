Vocabulary Words

  • lost

    Definition: Could not find something anymore.

    Example: I lost my red crayon, and now I can't finish my drawing.

  • between

    Definition: In the space that separates two things or places.

    Example: The little bird built its nest between two branches on the tree.

  • wall

    Definition: A solid side of a room or building.

    Example: We painted a colorful picture on the classroom wall.

  • helped

    Definition: Gave support or aid to someone.

    Example: My sister helped me clean my room before our friends came over.

  • behind

    Definition: At the back of something.

    Example: The mischievous squirrel hid its nuts behind the big oak tree.
