Vocabulary Words

  • survey

    Definition: A way to ask many people questions to find out what they think or like.

    Example: Our class took a survey to choose our favorite ice cream flavor.

  • dislike

    Definition: To not like something at all.

    Example: My little brother dislikes eating broccoli for dinner.

  • practise

    Definition: To do something many times so you can get better at it.

    Example: She needs to practise playing her guitar before the concert.

  • favourite

    Definition: The person, thing, or animal that you like the most.

    Example: My favourite toy is my big teddy bear.

  • bright

    Definition: Full of light, or a strong and happy color.

    Example: The sun was so bright that I had to squint my eyes.
