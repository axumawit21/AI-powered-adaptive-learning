Vocabulary Words

  • uniform

    Definition: Special clothes that people wear for a job or school.

    Example: The police officer wore a blue uniform.

  • breakfast

    Definition: The first meal you eat in the morning.

    Example: My favorite breakfast is cereal with milk.

  • leaves

    Definition: To go away from a place.

    Example: The bus leaves the station at ten o'clock.

  • lunch

    Definition: A meal you eat in the middle of the day.

    Example: I packed a peanut butter sandwich for lunch.

  • studies

    Definition: To read books or think about things to learn.

    Example: She studies her ABCs every day.

  • evening

    Definition: The time of day after the afternoon and before night.

    Example: We often read a story in the evening before bed.

  • dinner

    Definition: The main meal eaten in the evening.

    Example: For dinner, we had chicken and vegetables.
