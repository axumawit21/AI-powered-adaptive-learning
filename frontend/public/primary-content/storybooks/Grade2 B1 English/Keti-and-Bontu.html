Vocabulary Words

  • healthy

    Definition: Being strong and not sick.

    Example: Eating vegetables helps you stay healthy.

  • properly

    Definition: In the right way or correctly.

    Example: You should tie your shoelaces properly so you don't trip.

  • often

    Definition: Happening many times or frequently.

    Example: My cat often naps in the warmest spot in the house.

  • unhappy

    Definition: Feeling sad or not joyful.

    Example: She was unhappy because her favorite cartoon was over.

  • sick

    Definition: Not feeling well or having an illness.

    Example: I stayed home from school because I felt sick with a cold.

  • avocado

    Definition: A green, creamy fruit with a large seed inside.

    Example: I like to eat avocado on my toast in the morning.
