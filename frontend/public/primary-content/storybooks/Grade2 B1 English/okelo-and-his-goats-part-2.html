Vocabulary Words

  • pretends

    Definition: To act like something you are not, just for fun.

    Example: My little sister often pretends to be a princess.

  • waves

    Definition: To move your hand or arm back and forth.

    Example: The lifeguard waves a flag to show that swimming is safe.

  • swings

    Definition: To move back and forth, often while hanging from something.

    Example: The children love to swing high on the playground.

  • bumps

    Definition: To hit something gently or accidentally.

    Example: The small car bumps into the curb when parking.

  • best

    Definition: The most good or wonderful of all.

    Example: My mom makes the best chocolate chip cookies.
