Vocabulary Words

  • agrees

    Definition: When people think the same thing or say yes to an idea.

    Example: My brother always agrees with me when I suggest we play outside.

  • best

    Definition: The most good or the favorite one out of many.

    Example: My grandma makes the best chocolate chip cookies.

  • true

    Definition: Something that is real or correct, not pretend or wrong.

    Example: Is it true that elephants have long trunks?

  • asks

    Definition: To say something to find out information or get an answer.

    Example: The little bird asks its mommy for a worm to eat.

  • answers

    Definition: To say something back when someone asks you a question.

    Example: The smart student always answers the teacher's questions quickly.
