Vocabulary Words

  • sweep

    Definition: To clean the floor using a broom.

    Example: It's important to sweep up all the dirt after playing outside.

  • dishes

    Definition: Plates, bowls, and cups that you eat from.

    Example: After lunch, we put all the dirty dishes in the sink.

  • set

    Definition: To arrange plates, forks, and cups on a table before a meal.

    Example: Mom asked me to help set the table for dinner.

  • clothes

    Definition: Things you wear, like shirts, pants, and socks.

    Example: My new clothes are very colorful and soft.

  • feed

    Definition: To give food to a person or an animal.

    Example: Remember to feed the cat her breakfast every morning.
