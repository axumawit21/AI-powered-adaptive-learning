Vocabulary Words

  • village

    Definition: A very small town with not many houses.

    Example: My aunt lives in a quiet village far from the city.

  • sweep

    Definition: To clean the floor with a broom.

    Example: I helped my mom sweep the kitchen after dinner.

  • alone

    Definition: By yourself, with no one else.

    Example: The little bird was all alone on the branch.

  • grandfather

    Definition: Your mother's or father's dad.

    Example: My grandfather tells the best stories.

  • grandmother

    Definition: Your mother's or father's mom.

    Example: My grandmother bakes delicious cookies.
