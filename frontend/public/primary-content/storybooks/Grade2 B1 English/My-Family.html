Vocabulary Words

  • nephew

    Definition: The son of your brother or sister.

    Example: My older brother's son, Leo, is my nephew.

  • niece

    Definition: The daughter of your brother or sister.

    Example: My little niece loves to draw pictures for me.

  • uncle

    Definition: The brother of your mom or dad.

    Example: My uncle likes to tell funny jokes at family dinners.

  • aunt

    Definition: The sister of your mom or dad.

    Example: My aunt bakes the best cookies for my birthday.

  • office

    Definition: A room or building where people work, often at desks.

    Example: My dad goes to his office every morning to do his job.

  • grandfather

    Definition: The father of your mom or dad.

    Example: My grandfather reads me a bedtime story every time I visit.

  • grandmother

    Definition: The mother of your mom or dad.

    Example: My grandmother taught me how to knit a scarf.
