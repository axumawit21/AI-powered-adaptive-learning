Vocabulary Words

  • gathering

    Definition: Collecting many things together.

    Example: The children were gathering colorful leaves in the park.

  • firewood

    Definition: Pieces of wood that are used to make a fire.

    Example: We piled the firewood neatly beside the fireplace for the cold night.

  • stronger

    Definition: Having more power or force.

    Example: The little ant grew stronger every day by carrying tiny crumbs.

  • leads

    Definition: Shows the way or goes in front to guide others.

    Example: A captain always leads his ship through the rough seas.

  • animals

    Definition: Living creatures that move around and are not plants or people.

    Example: The farmer took good care of all his animals, including the cows and chickens.
