Vocabulary Words

  • between

    Definition: In the middle of two things.

    Example: The small dog walked between the two tall trees.

  • objects

    Definition: Things that you can see and touch.

    Example: There were many colorful objects on the toy shelf.

  • benches

    Definition: Long seats where several people can sit together.

    Example: We sat on the park benches to eat our lunch.

  • nailed

    Definition: Fastened or attached with a small metal pin called a nail.

    Example: The carpenter carefully nailed the wooden planks together.

  • chalk

    Definition: A soft white or colored stick used for writing on a board.

    Example: The children drew hopscotch squares on the pavement with a piece of chalk.

  • simple

    Definition: Easy to understand or do; not complicated.

    Example: The instructions for building the toy were very simple.
