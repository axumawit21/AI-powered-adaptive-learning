Vocabulary Words

  • older

    Definition: Having lived for more years than someone else.

    Example: My older sister helps me with my homework.

  • wearing

    Definition: Having clothes or jewelry on your body.

    Example: The girl is wearing a bright red hat.

  • skirt

    Definition: A piece of clothing that hangs from the waist and covers the legs.

    Example: She twirled around in her new blue skirt.

  • sweater

    Definition: A warm piece of clothing for the top part of your body, often made of wool.

    Example: I put on a soft sweater to stay warm in the cold weather.

  • trousers

    Definition: A piece of clothing that covers your legs separately, like pants.

    Example: My dad wears his smart trousers when he goes to work.

  • shirts

    Definition: Pieces of clothing for the upper part of the body, often with sleeves and a collar.

    Example: He has many different colored shirts in his closet.
