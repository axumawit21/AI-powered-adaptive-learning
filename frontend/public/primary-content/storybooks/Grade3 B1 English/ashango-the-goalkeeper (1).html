Vocabulary Words

  • Goalkeeper

    Definition: The player in a game like soccer who guards the goal to stop the other team from scoring.

    Example: The amazing goalkeeper jumped high to save the ball.

  • Teammates

    Definition: The friends you play on the same sports team with.

    Example: My teammates cheered loudly when I scored a goal.

  • Grade

    Definition: A school year or level that a student is in.

    Example: My sister is in first grade, and I am in third grade.

  • Break time

    Definition: A short rest time, often at school, where you can play or eat.

    Example: During break time, we go outside to play tag.

  • Catches

    Definition: To grab or get hold of something that is moving through the air.

    Example: My dog loves to catch the frisbee when I throw it.
