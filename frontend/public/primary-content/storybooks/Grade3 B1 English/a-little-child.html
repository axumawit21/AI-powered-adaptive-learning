Vocabulary Words

  • Care

    Definition: To look after someone or something and keep them safe and healthy.

    Example: My older sister takes good care of her pet hamster.

  • Sometimes

    Definition: Not always, but only at certain moments or on certain days.

    Example: Sometimes, it rains even when the sun is shining.

  • Clinic

    Definition: A small hospital or doctor's office where people go when they are not feeling well.

    Example: The nurse at the clinic gave me a sticker after my check-up.

  • Family

    Definition: A group of people who are related to each other, like parents, children, and sometimes grandparents.

    Example: My whole family gathered for a big picnic in the park.

  • Hungry

    Definition: Feeling a need to eat food.

    Example: After playing outside all morning, the puppy was very hungry.
