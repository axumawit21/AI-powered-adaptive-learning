Vocabulary Words

  • loudly

    Definition: Making a lot of noise that can be heard easily.

    Example: The baby laughed loudly when his mom played peek-a-boo.

  • classroom

    Definition: A room in a school where students learn together.

    Example: We listened to the teacher read a story in the classroom.

  • timetable

    Definition: A plan that shows when different things will happen, like lessons at school.

    Example: My dance class has a timetable so I know what time to arrive.

  • periods

    Definition: Specific blocks of time during the school day for different subjects or activities.

    Example: We have three different periods before it's time for lunch.

  • following

    Definition: Doing what someone says or obeying rules.

    Example: The children were following the rules of the game carefully.
