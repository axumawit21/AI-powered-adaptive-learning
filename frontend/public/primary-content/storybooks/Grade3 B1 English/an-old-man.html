Vocabulary Words

  • confused

    Definition: Not understanding something clearly or feeling mixed up.

    Example: The puppy looked confused when the ball rolled under the couch.

  • market

    Definition: A place where people go to buy and sell things, especially food.

    Example: We went to the market to buy fresh fruits and vegetables.

  • town

    Definition: A place where people live, usually bigger than a village but smaller than a city.

    Example: Our small town has one main street with many shops.

  • waits

    Definition: Stays in one place until something happens or someone arrives.

    Example: The cat waits by the door for its owner to come inside.

  • daughter

    Definition: A girl or woman who is someone's child.

    Example: The queen's daughter loves to ride her horse.

  • together

    Definition: When people or things are with each other or connected.

    Example: The friends built a tall sandcastle together at the beach.
