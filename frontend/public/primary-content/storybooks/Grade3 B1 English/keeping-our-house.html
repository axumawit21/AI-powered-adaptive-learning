Vocabulary Words

  • collect

    Definition: To gather things together in one place.

    Example: We like to collect seashells when we go to the beach.

  • waste basket

    Definition: A container where you throw away trash or things you don't need anymore.

    Example: Please put the crumpled paper into the waste basket.

  • sweeps

    Definition: To clean the floor or ground with a broom.

    Example: My dad sweeps the garage floor every weekend.

  • broom

    Definition: A cleaning tool with a long handle and bristles, used for sweeping floors.

    Example: I used a small broom to sweep the dust under my bed.

  • ceiling

    Definition: The top part inside a room, above your head.

    Example: There is a light fixture attached to the ceiling.

  • twice

    Definition: Two times.

    Example: I like to eat pancakes twice a week for breakfast.
