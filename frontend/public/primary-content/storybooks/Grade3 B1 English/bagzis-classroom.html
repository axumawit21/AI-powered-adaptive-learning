Vocabulary Words

  • primary

    Definition: First or main; like the first kind of school you go to.

    Example: My little brother started going to primary school this year.

  • revises

    Definition: Looks at something again to check it or make it better.

    Example: Before the big test, I always revises my notes to remember everything.

  • previous

    Definition: Happening or coming before something else.

    Example: The previous day, we went to the park and played for hours.

  • corrections

    Definition: Changes made to fix mistakes.

    Example: The teacher made some corrections on my spelling test, so I could learn from them.

  • regularly

    Definition: Doing something often or at set times.

    Example: We regularly visit the library to borrow new books every Saturday.

  • participates

    Definition: To join in or be part of an activity with others.

    Example: My sister always participates in the school plays because she loves to act.

  • activities

    Definition: Things you do for fun or to learn.

    Example: During summer camp, we do many fun activities like swimming and crafting.
