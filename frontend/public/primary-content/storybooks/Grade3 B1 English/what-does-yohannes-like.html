Vocabulary Words

  • born

    Definition: When a baby comes into the world.

    Example: My little brother was born in the spring.

  • lives

    Definition: Where someone makes their home.

    Example: A bird lives in a nest in the tree.

  • guitar

    Definition: A musical instrument with strings that you can strum or pluck.

    Example: She learned to play a happy song on her new guitar.

  • porridge

    Definition: A warm, soft breakfast food made from cooked grains, like oatmeal.

    Example: On cold mornings, I like to eat hot porridge with honey.

  • dislikes

    Definition: To not like something; to think it is unpleasant.

    Example: My dog dislikes taking a bath, but he loves playing in the mud.

  • sodas

    Definition: Sweet, bubbly drinks often called soft drinks.

    Example: For her birthday party, she chose orange sodas for everyone to drink.
