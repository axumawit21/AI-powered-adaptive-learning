Vocabulary Words

  • Duties

    Definition: Jobs or tasks you have to do regularly.

    Example: It is my duty to help set the table for dinner every night.

  • Student

    Definition: A person who is learning at a school or college.

    Example: The new student was very excited for their first day in class.

  • Prepare

    Definition: To get something ready.

    Example: Mom likes to prepare our lunchboxes the night before school.

  • Uniform

    Definition: A special set of clothes that all people in a group wear.

    Example: The soccer team wore their blue and white uniform to the game.

  • Usually

    Definition: Most of the time, or almost always.

    Example: My dog usually barks when the mailman comes to the door.
