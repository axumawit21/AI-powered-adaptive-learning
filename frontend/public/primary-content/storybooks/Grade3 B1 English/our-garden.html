Vocabulary Words

  • hoe

    Definition: A garden tool with a long handle and a flat blade, used for digging or removing weeds.

    Example: My dad uses a hoe to prepare the soil for planting flowers.

  • seedlings

    Definition: Very young plants that have just started to grow from a seed.

    Example: We carefully moved the tiny tomato seedlings into bigger pots.

  • weeds

    Definition: Wild plants that grow where they are not wanted, often taking space and food from desired plants.

    Example: It's important to pull out the weeds so our carrots can grow big and strong.

  • proud

    Definition: Feeling pleased and happy about something good you have done or something you own.

    Example: My brother felt very proud when he finished building his tall block tower.

  • digging

    Definition: Making a hole in the ground by moving dirt away.

    Example: The dog was digging a hole in the yard to bury its bone.

  • planting

    Definition: Putting seeds or young plants into the ground so they can grow.

    Example: We spent the afternoon planting colorful flowers all around the house.
