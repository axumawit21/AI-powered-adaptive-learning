Vocabulary Words

  • corrects

    Definition: To fix something that is wrong.

    Example: The teacher corrects our homework so we can learn from our errors.

  • mistakes

    Definition: Something you do or say that is not right.

    Example: Everyone makes mistakes sometimes, and that's how we learn new things.

  • advises

    Definition: To tell someone what you think they should do.

    Example: My older brother advises me to eat my vegetables so I can grow strong.

  • obey

    Definition: To do what someone tells you to do.

    Example: It is important for pets to obey their owners for their safety.

  • important

    Definition: Something that matters a lot or is very useful.

    Example: It is important to wear your seatbelt when you are in the car.
