Vocabulary Words

  • Family

    Definition: A group of people who are related to each other, like parents, children, and siblings.

    Example: My family loves to go on picnics in the park.

  • Grandmother

    Definition: The mother of your mother or father.

    Example: My grandmother taught me how to knit a scarf.

  • Aunt

    Definition: The sister of your mother or father.

    Example: My aunt brought me a souvenir from her trip.

  • Live

    Definition: To have your home in a particular place.

    Example: Many animals live in the forest.

  • Stories

    Definition: Tales or accounts of events that can be real or imagined.

    Example: I enjoy listening to funny stories before bedtime.
