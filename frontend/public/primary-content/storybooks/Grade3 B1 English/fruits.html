Vocabulary Words

  • common

    Definition: Something you see or find very often.

    Example: Dogs and cats are common pets in many homes.

  • thick

    Definition: Not thin; having a big distance from one side to the other.

    Example: The book had a thick cover to protect its pages.

  • unripe

    Definition: Not ready to eat yet, usually green.

    Example: The green apple was unripe and tasted sour.

  • ripe

    Definition: Ready to eat, usually sweet and juicy.

    Example: We waited for the strawberries to turn red and ripe before picking them.

  • peel

    Definition: To take off the skin or outer layer of something.

    Example: My mom taught me how to peel a potato safely.

  • curved

    Definition: Bent or rounded, not straight.

    Example: The rainbow made a beautiful curved shape in the sky.
