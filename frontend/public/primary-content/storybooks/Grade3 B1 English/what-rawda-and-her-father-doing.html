Vocabulary Words

  • compound

    Definition: An area of land with buildings inside it, often surrounded by a fence or wall.

    Example: The children played soccer in the school compound during recess.

  • gardener

    Definition: A person whose job is to take care of a garden and grow plants.

    Example: My grandma is a great gardener; her flowers are always beautiful.

  • digs

    Definition: Pushes into the ground with hands or tools to make a hole or move dirt.

    Example: The dog often digs holes in the backyard to bury its toys.

  • plant

    Definition: To put a seed or a young plant in the ground so it can grow.

    Example: We decided to plant a small tree in our garden to help the environment.

  • colourful

    Definition: Full of bright colors or having many different colors.

    Example: The artist used colourful paints to create a vibrant picture.

  • grade

    Definition: A level or year in school.

    Example: My brother is in the first grade, and he loves learning to read.

  • early

    Definition: Happening or done before the usual or expected time.

    Example: We woke up early on Saturday to go fishing.
